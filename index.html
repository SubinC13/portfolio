RCAS - School Website:

         The RCAS School Website is a comprehensive online platform that provides detailed information about the school, its activities, history, and contact details. It serves as a central hub for students, parents, teachers, and the community to access essential information about the school. The website offers a user-friendly interface, allowing visitors to navigate through different sections such as academics, extracurricular activities, admissions, and faculty profiles. It also showcases the school's achievements, events, and showcases student work.

SHESAFE - Women Safety Application :

         SHESAFE is a women's safety application designed to provide immediate assistance in emergency situations. It offers features like SOS alerts, where users can send distress signals to their emergency contacts with just a single tap. The app also includes real-time location tracking, allowing designated contacts to monitor the user's whereabouts in case of an emergency. SHESAFE empowers women to feel safer and more secure, providing them with a reliable tool for personal safety.

Drug Identification Project:

         The Drug Identification Project is a Python-based application that utilizes the OpenCV library to identify individuals who may be under the influence of drugs. This innovative project leverages computer vision and image processing techniques to analyze facial cues and patterns that are indicative of drug consumption. By capturing real-time video or images, the system applies algorithms to detect specific physiological and behavioral changes associated with drug use. The project aims to assist law enforcement agencies, rehabilitation centers, or organizations working in drug prevention to identify potential cases of substance abuse, enabling timely intervention and support for individuals in need.

Hozon - Android Application:

         Hozon is an Android application designed to tackle food wastage by connecting individuals or businesses with surplus food to those in need. The app allows users to post listings of leftover food items that they want to donate. People looking for food can browse these listings and request the available items. Hozon aims to promote sustainable living by reducing food waste and providing assistance to those who are in need of meals.
